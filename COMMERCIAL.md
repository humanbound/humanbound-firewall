## Option 1 — AGPL-3.0-only (free, open-source)

The default license is [AGPL-3.0-only](./LICENSE). It grants you broad rights
to use, modify, and redistribute the software, with one notable obligation:

> **If you run a modified version as a network service that interacts with
> users, you must make your modified source code available to those users
> under AGPL-3.0 as well.**

The AGPL is well-suited to:

- Individual developers and internal tools
- Academic research and open-source projects
- Organizations whose downstream product is already open-source
- Evaluation, proofs of concept, and non-commercial use

## Option 2 — Commercial license (paid, proprietary)

The commercial license removes the AGPL's network-copyleft obligations. You
may embed `humanbound-firewall` in closed-source products and operate
networked services using it without being required to release your source
code. Suitable for:

- Enterprises shipping `humanbound-firewall` inside a closed-source product
- SaaS vendors operating commercial services that incorporate the firewall
- Organizations whose legal or compliance requirements preclude AGPL
- Users who want indemnification, priority support, or a signed agreement

| Your situation | License |

| Internal tools, hobby projects, academic research | AGPL-3.0 (free) |
| Your product is already AGPL or GPL-3.0-compatible | AGPL-3.0 (free) |
| You're forking or running as a SaaS and OK to release your modifications | AGPL-3.0 (free) |
| You're embedding in a closed-source enterprise product | Commercial |
